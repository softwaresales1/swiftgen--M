Get Verified to Receive Payments

Complete identity verification to start receiving payments securely through PayPal.

Why Verify?
Verification ensures secure transactions and builds trust with freelancers. It's required to receive payments on our platform.

Verification Requirements

  • Government-issued ID (Passport, Driver's License, or National ID)
  • Proof of address (Utility bill or bank statement - within 3 months)
  • Selfie with your ID document
  • Valid PayPal account for receiving payments
  • Business documents (if applying as a business)

PayPal Integration

We use PayPal for secure payment processing. You'll need to provide your PayPal email address during verification. Once approved, you'll receive payments directly to your PayPal account.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?

Typically 2-3 business days. We'll notify you via email once your verification is reviewed.

What documents are accepted?

Government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, national ID card) and recent proof of address.

Is my information secure?

Yes, all verification data is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with international data protection standards.
