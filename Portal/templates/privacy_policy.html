Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information

Last Updated: September 21, 2025
Your Privacy Matters: SwiftTalentForge is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparent data practices. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our services, we collect information you voluntarily provide:

Information Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Name, username, email, password, phone number Account creation and authentication
Profile Information Bio, skills, experience, portfolio, profile picture Professional matching and discovery
Financial Information Payment methods, billing address, tax information Payment processing and compliance
Communication Data Messages, project communications, support tickets Service delivery and support
Project Information Project descriptions, requirements, deliverables Facilitating work relationships

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our platform, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform, click patterns
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Performance Data: Error reports, loading times, platform performance metrics

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Payment Processors: Transaction data from Stripe, PayPal, and other payment providers
  • Identity Verification Services: Information to verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Social Media: If you connect social media accounts (with your permission)
  • Public Sources: Publicly available information to verify credentials

2How We Use Your Information

Legal Basis: We process your information based on legitimate business interests, contractual necessity, legal compliance, and your consent where required.

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Service Provision: Creating accounts, facilitating connections, processing payments
  • Platform Security: Fraud prevention, content moderation, security monitoring
  • Communication: Sending notifications, updates, and support responses
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance platform features
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and regulatory requirements

2.2 Marketing and Communications

With your consent, we may use your information for:

  • Sending promotional emails about new features or services
  • Providing personalized recommendations
  • Inviting participation in surveys or feedback programs
  • Sharing relevant industry insights and tips

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

3Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 When We Share Information

We only share your information in specific circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Profile information visible to facilitate business connections
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help operate our platform
  • Payment Processing: Financial institutions and payment processors for transactions
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or regulatory request
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • Safety and Security: To protect users, prevent fraud, or address security issues

3.2 What We Never Share

We Never:
  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share private messages without legal requirement
  • Disclose financial information beyond transaction necessities
  • Provide contact information to unauthorized parties

4Data Security and Protection

4.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls and authentication
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Secure, encrypted backups with redundancy

4.2 Payment Security

Financial information is protected through:

  • PCI DSS compliant payment processors
  • Tokenization of payment information
  • Fraud detection and prevention systems
  • Secure escrow services for project payments
